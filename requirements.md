# Lida Education Consulting Website Requirements

## 1. Overview
**Purpose:**  
Create a professional, informative, and visually appealing **static website** that introduces Lida Education Consulting’s services, philosophy, and success stories to prospective students and parents.  

**Target Audience:**  
- International students aiming to apply to universities in the **U.S., Canada, and Australia**  
- Parents seeking professional education consulting for their children  
- Students preparing for exams like **IELTS, TOEFL, GRE, or GMAT**

**Primary Goals:**  
- Present company information and services clearly  
- Build credibility through success cases and professional background  
- Encourage potential clients to reach out via contact form or email  

---

## 2. Website Structure

### **Home Page**
- Overview of Lida Education Consulting  
- Hero banner with slogan (e.g., “Empowering Global Education Dreams”)  
- Quick links to services, language training, and success cases  
- Highlights of company strengths (personalized consulting, expert advisors, global network)  

---

### **About Us**
- **Mission & Vision:** Provide customized educational guidance and bridge resource gaps for students  
- **Core Philosophy:** “己欲立立人，己欲达达人” — helping others succeed through personalized education  
- **Team Overview:**  
  - Over 20 experienced consultants and educators  
  - Backgrounds from top universities (Ivy League, G5, Canada Top 3)  
  - Overseas education and work experience  
- **Location:** Vancouver, Canada (with global educational policy expertise)

---

### **Our Services**
Organized into three sections:

#### 1. **Application Consulting**
- Personalized school selection plans  
- Analysis of school ranking, location, and employment outcomes  
- Scholarship and tuition analysis  
- Transparent application management (timeline, documentation, submission tracking)

#### 2. **Background Enhancement**
- Internship placement and academic research opportunities  
- Research assistant and international conference participation  
- Guidance on resume and recommendation letter preparation  
- Cultural exchange and short-term study abroad experiences

#### 3. **Training & Preparation**
- Language exam training (IELTS, TOEFL, Duolingo)  
- GRE & GMAT test preparation  
- Interview skill coaching  
- Undergraduate study planning (for Year 3–4 students)

---

### **Success Stories**
- Visual gallery of admission offers from top universities (Ivy League, G5, Canada Top 3, Group of Eight, etc.)  
- Summary of achievements (e.g., improved acceptance rates, top-ranked schools)  
- Testimonials from students and parents (optional)

---

### **Contact Us**
- Contact form (name, email, message)  
- Email, phone, and WeChat QR code (optional)  
- Office location (Vancouver, Canada)  
- Embedded Google Map  

---

## 3. Design Requirements
- **Style:** Clean, professional, and international aesthetic  
- **Color Scheme:** Blue and white (education and trust theme)  
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif font (e.g., Open Sans, Lato)  
- **Layout:**  
  - Responsive (desktop and mobile)  
  - Consistent navigation bar and footer  
- **Media:**  
  - Professional photos (team, students, universities)  
  - Icons for clarity in service sections  

---

## 4. Technical Requirements
- **Type:** Static website (no backend interaction)  
- **Technology:** HTML5, CSS3, and React
- **Hosting:** Compatible with any standard static hosting (e.g., GitHub Pages, Netlify)  
- **Languages:**  
  - English (primary)  
  - Optional Chinese version for bilingual accessibility  

---

## 5. SEO & Content Requirements
- Meta descriptions for all pages  
- Alt text for images  
- Keywords: “education consulting,” “study abroad,” “university application,” “IELTS training,” “Canada,” “US,” “Australia”  
- Structured titles and headings (H1–H3) for better indexing
